About Lo-QOS

Lo-QOS aims to create a revolutionary operating system that bridges classical and quantum computing. Our vision is to develop a platform that makes quantum computing accessible while leveraging AI and blockchain for enhanced security and functionality.

Project Goals

  • Create an open-source quantum-classical hybrid OS
  • Develop accessible quantum computing tools
  • Build a secure, AI-enhanced system architecture
  • Foster a collaborative development community
