Executive Summary

Mission: To provide high-quality, sustainably packaged sugar products while building a resilient business that empowers our community.

Resilient Negroar is a Louisiana-based sugar repackaging and distribution company, strategically positioned to serve both local and national markets. We aim to capture a significant share of the $12 billion U.S. sugar market through innovative packaging, sustainable practices, and efficient distribution.
